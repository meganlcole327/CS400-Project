About Our Product

Play It Forward is a toy exchange and donation platform that connects people seeking to sell, donate, or trade toys. Focused on fostering learning and creativity, the app allows users to browse toys in various age categories, such as ages 0-1, 1-3, 3-5, 6-9, and 10-12 years old. With location-based searches, the platform ensures that toys can be exchanged within local communities, promoting sustainability and making toys accessible to all.

Play It Forward is designed for parents, caregivers, and individuals looking to pass on or acquire gently used toys. The platform caters to people seeking educational toys for younger children and focuses on connecting them with toys that support cognitive and physical development. Non-profit organizations can also benefit by receiving toy donations or distributing toys to families in need, creating a bridge between individuals and those who require toys for children in their care.

Features and Benefits

Localized Search

Users can search for toys avaliable for donation, trade, or purchase within the specific neighborhood or area, making it easier to connect with others nearby while supporting local families and organizations.

Age-Specific Toy Listings

Users are able to browse toys tailored to different age ranges (e.g., 0-1, 1-3, 3-5, 6-9, 10-12), which ensures for a more targeted and relevants experience for our users.

Donation Focus

Play It Forward will focus on donations and trades over sales, with dedicated sections for users to donate toys to non-profits. Non-profits can advertise to receive toy donations, helping users to support families and organizations in need.

Product Vision

Play It Forward seeks to make sure all children have the appropriate toys to foster learning throughout childhood. Play It Forward seeks to unite those wishing to get rid of toys their children have grown out of with those seeking to find new toys for their younger children to play with. All toys listed are in usable condition with plenty of life and love left in them. Play It Forward focuses on toys played with and loved by younger ages with a primary focus on learning-type toys to provide educational opportunities for those who may not have as many toys intended for learning. Play It Forward also will lean on non-profit organizations - as donatees and donators - to help fill the gaps of toys needing to be properly placed.
